Macaroni Salad

Macaroni salad is a popular Filipino dessert made with macaroni, mayonnaise, and fruit. It's a classic dessert that's easy to make and perfect for any occasion.

  • Ingredients:

  • 1 cup macaroni
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 cup fruit cocktail
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • Salt and pepper to taste

  • Instructions:

    • Cook the macaroni according to package instructions.
    • Mix the cooked macaroni with mayonnaise, fruit cocktail, and chopped onion.
    • Season with salt and pepper to taste.
