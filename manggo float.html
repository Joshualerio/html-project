Manggo Float

Ingredients:

  • 5 manggos
  • 4 cups heavy cream
  • 1 can sweetend condinsed milk
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 21 graham crackers

Instructions

  1. Mix cream: combined condinsed milk,Evaporate milk heavy cream, and optional yogurt and vanilla in a bowl.
  2. Layer: In a dish, alternate layers of graham crackers, mango slices, and the cream mixture. Repeat layers and finish with cream on top.
  3. Serve: Garnish with extra mango slices and mint leaves if desired.
