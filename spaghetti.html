Healthy spaghetti

Spaghetti is a Filipino-style dessert made with sweetened spaghetti, milk, and sugar. It's a unique and delicious dessert that's perfect for hot summer days.

  • Ingredients:

  • 1 cup sweetened spaghetti
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

  • Instructions:

      • Cook the sweetened spaghetti according to package instructions.
      • Mix the cooked spaghetti with milk, sugar, and salt.
      • And add fresh basil
      • Chill in the refrigerator before serving.
