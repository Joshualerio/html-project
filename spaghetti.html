Healthy spaghetti

  • Ingredients:

    1. 1 cup sweetened spaghetti
    2. 1 cup milk
    3. 1/2 cup sugar
    4. 1/4 teaspoon salt

  • Instructions:

    1. Cook the sweetened spaghetti according to package instructions.
    2. Mix the cooked spaghetti with milk, sugar, and salt.
    3. And add fresh basil
    4. Chill in the refrigerator before serving.

    Spaghetti is a Filipino-style dessert made with sweetened spaghetti, milk, and sugar. It's a unique and delicious dessert that's perfect for hot summer days.
